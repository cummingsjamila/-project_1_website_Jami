After dreams are categorised into their divisions they can be categorised into up to 7 minor divisions. The seven minor divisions dreams can be classified as are as follows:Psychological Healing Dreams, Belief Dreams,Problem-Solving Dreams,Physiological Dreams,Dreams of Daily Life,Compensatory Dreams,and Recurring Dreams.

Nightmares

Nightmares are common in young-children and adults. Often nightmares are caused by: Stress, conflict, fear, trauma, emotional problems, medication/drug use, and illness. If you feel as though have a recurring nightmare(s), your subconscious may be trying to tell you something.Alway listen to it, for it’s telling you for a reason. If you can't figure out what’s the cause behind your bad dreams, and you continue to have them, talk to a doctor. Having frequent nightmares can be bad for your health causing you to become restless,anxious, and paranoid.Talking to doctors may help you figure out what is causing your nightmares. Always keep in mind that no matter how scary a nightmare is, it is not real.

Lucid Dreaming

Have you ever had a dream where you knew you were dreaming while in a dream state? This is called a lucid dreaming. Research has shown that lucid dreaming is accompanied by an increased activation of parts of the brain that are normally not active during your REM sleep. Lucid dreaming represents a brain state between REM sleep and being awake.Some people who are lucid dreamers are able to influence the direction of their dream. Therefore they are able to change what is going on in their dream. Think of it like being in a video game except there isn’t a pre-made storyline; just you controlling what will and when things will happen. While this may be a good tactic to take on during a nightmare, it’s better for you let your dreams occur naturally Dream experts do not suport lucid dreaming because there isn’t a lot of research on the issue, therefore the cons are not well-known.

This is basically the process of putting the phrase “Just sleep on it” to the test. The concept behind this is to just think of an issue/topic that it bothering you. Then go to sleep and when you wake up you should have a solution. It isn’t the best forming of dreaming seeming how you have to a have a bit of control of your dreams but not to much.
