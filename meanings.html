WHAT DO OUR DREAMS MEAN?

Dreaming itself is a very convoluted concept. The way most people underestand it, is that when we go to sleep perhaps with a certain topic/emotion on our mind,we then have vivid images which relect these things. This concept is know as dreaming. Now if you are anything like me and are curious about everything, then I'm sure you have wondered what they mean!

IMPORTANT DREAM SYMBOLS TO LOOK FOR

The first step to being able to interpret dreams is to know what the most important symbols are within them. Since there are so many in the list I have decided to only list the top five. If you would like to know more simply click on one of the links below.

  1. BEING CHASED

    Being chased is one of the most commonly reported dreams. Because it is such a vivid emotion we tend to be able to remember the whole dream itself. Many experts believe that chase dreams mean we may not be addressing something in our lives that requries attention.

  2. WATER DREAMS

    It's said that water typically reprsents our inner emoitions or our unconscious minds. The condition of the water vivid bright ocean water or cloudy, dark, rain water often helps to represent emotions and mental health.

  3. VECHICLES/DEATH

    Wether it's a car, an airplane, train, ship, anything you can travel in, know that in dreams the vehcile you travel in can relfect the road in which we are taking, and how much we have control over our current situtations. This can help show us the obstacles we have already faced or the ones we have yet to overcome.

  4. PEOPLE

    When you see people in your dreams it can reflect the different aspects of the self. The people in our dreams can also relate to the goals we want to achive, as well as the traits we desire most. Another thing people can represent is love. Have a crush on anyone? Well if so, or even if you say no, if that special person appears in your dreams you might need to think again. Every person you see or envision in a dream has some value to you and your life matters.

  5. FALLING/FLYING

    Flying in a dream depending on how well it's done, relates to how much control we have over our lives. If you are flying well, and soaring high you are a very confident person and achieve many of your goals. If you are falling in a dream it's not always a bad thing! Some experts believe that it means that when you are falling in slow motion you a releasing toxic energy and better times are ahead. However if you feel as though you're falling from a high hieght perhaps you are losing control over things in your life.
