The History of Dreams

In ancient society dreams were very important. Dreams influenced our ancestors, their decisions they make in their everyday lives. How do we know this you may wonder?

Ancient texts such as the bible are filled with tons of references to people having vivid visions that provide them with the answers to their daily problems!

Why we dream and other theories!

There are many,many,many,theories as to why we dream. A major theory was created by Sigmund Feud that was widely popular in the 19th century. He believed that dreams gave us access to our unconscious inner conflicts. He called them “ the royal road to the knowledge on the part that the unconscious plays in mental life. Another theory that came about a few years later was created by Alfred Adler. Alfred Adler believed that “dreams reflect the current lifestyle, and offer solutions to current contemporary conflicts”. Research on dreams started to become popular around the same time that research on the REM ( rapid eye movement) started to become well known as well.
